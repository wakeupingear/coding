Project-Based Approach

Learning Computer Science is so fun because of the things that computers enable you to create.

But by working through projects - games, websites, or apps - you gain the knowledge to do even more.

Understand Computers

At Coding with Will, we approach CS as a powerful tool to create. But, we also focus on the core technology and principles that power those projects.

Many tutoring groups will walk you through making something. But too often, they fail to teach you HOW you can continue after the class has ended.

With us, you'll walk out of the class with a project and a plan for the future. You'll have the skills to create on your own and the work product to prove it.

So don't just play with computers.

Make with computers.
